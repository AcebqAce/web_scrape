About Armada

Armada is a Zurich-based entrepreneurial driven investment firm. Fully owned and lead by Daniel S. Aegerter, Armada provides founder capital to ambitious and visionary entrepreneurs. Founded in 2000, we are pursuing the most promising companies and investments in private equity, public market securities and real estate. For the past 20 years we have been fortunate to back some unique ideas by visionary founders that turned into great companies.

Daniel S. Aegerter

Daniel S. Aegerter is a Swiss entrepreneur and Founder/CEO of Armada. He works with the founders of companies in Armada’s venture portfolio and has taken an active role in launching several new companies over the last 30 years. 

 

He founded his first company age 18 and built one of the earliest digital B2B marketplaces, Tradex Technologies, which was ultimately exited to Ariba in 2000 (Ariba was acquired by SAP in 2012). At the time of the exit, Tradex Technologies was one of the largest software acquisitions ever at a valuation of $5.6bn. Aged 31, Daniel went on to establish Armada as his private investment firm dedicated to back visionary founders.

 

«As a founder you need capital to build your company. But even more important is an experienced, straightforward capital partner to help you navigate through choppy waters and shifting winds. Having been a founder myself, this is the role I seek when backing founders as an investor today.»

 

Daniel is a philanthropist dedicated to power modern civilization through sustainable innovation.

 

A keynote presentation from December 2021, titled “Enabling a world I want to live in”, provides a glimpse into Daniel`s journey as a founder turned investor. You can follow the keynote as well as the ensuing interview with Steve Jurvetson here and here.

Investments

FOUNDER CAPITAL

Daniel’s approach to venture capital is shaped by his own entrepreneurial journey as a founder and CEO of a high growth technology company.

 

«I back founders the way I wanted to be backed.»

 

We invest in and partner with visionary entrepreneurs that work on massive problems and meaningful innovation. We believe in founders that tackle big questions with unique determination, humility at the task, and unwavering focus. 

 

«My investments also reflect some of my core beliefs and principles in life. I love working with founders that enable a future I would want to live in.»

 

A clear articulation of the nature of the problem and the ability to offer a realistic perspective on resource requirements for each stage of development is what gives us comfort that a grand vision can become a kick-ass product or service offering. Our investments are also guided by a humble awareness of underlying shifts in markets and regulatory paradigms. 

 

We offer our companies a broad range of support, most importantly leadership and decision coaching at times when it matters most. We also offer access to our broad network of partners that includes top venture capital and private equity firms across the globe.

 

We are happy to partner with visionary entrepreneurs all over the world, but particularly love backing founders in Switzerland, Germany, the UK and the United States. We invest in all stages but spend the majority of our time working with early stage teams.
