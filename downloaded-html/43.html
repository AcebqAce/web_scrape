Our Companies

We work with the entrepreneurs who are disrupting the way we design, build, operate, and experience our physical world. Whether they are democratizing design, augmenting the jobsite, or delivering the renewable energy and connectivity required to run it all, these are the revolutionary companies that are ushering in a better built world.

Animated Insights

Animated Insights is a leading provider for digital twin technology solutions across industry and infrastructure. By leveraging best in class technology and management services, Animated Insights bring digital twins to life.

assemble

Blokable

Blokable offers the world’s first vertically integrated development platform — from design, planning, financing, and permitting, to almost-complete manufacturing, delivery, on-site construction, and ongoing operational support.

Built Robotics’ mission is to build the robots that build the world. As the inventor of the world’s first autonomy solution for excavators, the Exosystem™, Built transforms excavators into fully autonomous trenching robots.

CANOA

CANOA helps brands to roll out space at scale, while drastically reducing the costs and environmental impact typically associated with expanding a real estate portfolio.

Dandelion

Dyn

Elephant Energy

enVerid

Envista

Powering the most innovative governments and their delivery of digital services to more than 275 million citizens worldwide.

Envoy

Envoy is a community-based shared mobility platform providing on-demand electric vehicles to apartments, workplaces, and hotels: Mobility as an Amenity™.

Extracker

Extracker is construction’s only change order communication platform. We help subcontractors, general contractors, and owners communicate project change order requests and time and material tags in an easy-to-use collaborative cloud-based log so companies can always be on the same page.

Fieldlens

A mobile and social project management platform for the building construction jobsite.

GoContractor

Digital onboarding and subcontractor management platform designed to get construction workers on site faster, reduce compliance risk, and improve safety, while driving productivity and profitability.

Honest Buildings

Data-driven project management and procurement platform for real estate owners.

Hypar

Icovia

An online space planning solutions to retailers, manufacturers, interior designers, and other design professionals.

Join
