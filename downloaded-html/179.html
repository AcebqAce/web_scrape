Our Investment
Strategy

It takes more than capital to develop the companies of tomorrow. We stay on the outlook for new trends in order to support entrepreneurs and growing projects, reduce as early as possible the risks associated with the execution of the business plan and industrial scale-up to reach the stages and conditions favorable to success, both from a financial and sustainability point of view.

WE DEPLOY OUR EXPERTISE TO DEVELOP SUSTAINABLE GLOBAL COMPANIES

Our investment strategy identifies and targets companies led by talented teams with high growth potential. These companies develop innovative intellectual property and cutting-edge technology for large markets. At the heart of our investment strategy is our ambition to invest in companies that create impact and help build the sustainable world of tomorrow. We systematically combine the financial and organizational analysis of companies with an analysis of the impacts produced by the company and the technology it develops.
