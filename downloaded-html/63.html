New Impact Funds in Europe in 2022

For over a year now, we’ve been monitoring the fundraising activities of impact-focused funds and publishing an overview of new ones on our Impact Shakers LinkedIn page every 2 weeks.

In this article, we assembled the ones that had a closing in 2022, offering an easy-to-scroll format, with important data points like ‘investment stage and geographical focus’ that can help impact startups find well suited investors. This information is intended to help founders but clearly, it cannot replace thorough research and due diligence.

Indico Capital Partners (PT)

Indico Capital Partners is an independent venture capital firm in Portugal investing in global technology companies. They announced the launch of its first climate action fund — the Indico Blue Fund — a €50 million, fully private capital venture and growth equity fund targeting Portuguese Blue Economy startups and SMEs. The Fund will invest from pre-seed to growth stages in innovative, sustainable solutions that have a positive, measurable contribution towards the Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs), in particular SDG 14 — Life underwater.

Fund size: €50m
Closing: closed in Jan 2022
Geographical focus: Portugal
Focus: tech startups and ocean-related startups and SMEs (blue economy)
Stage: pre-seed to Series A
Ticket size: €100k to €5m
Previous investments: Inclita, Kencko, Ittinsect (see full portfolio here)
Website: https://www.indicocapital.com/

How to pitch? Fill in a form or send an email
