Startup Profile Database

Welcome to BuiltWorlds Startup Profile Database! This venture-focused library is a compilation of well-positioned early-stage businesses, aiming to provide a lens on a company's solution/product, market opportunity/risks, leadership assessment, and other investor-focused information curated specifically for our Venture and Strategic members. BuiltWorlds Enterprise+ and Strategic members are able to obtain access to a set amount of startup profiles per quarter. 

The Startup Profile Database is sponsored by the BuiltWorlds Venture Forum, a group of forward-thinking member companies looking to drive innovation by both investing & piloting emerging technologies in the space and supporting the broader buildings and infrastructure technology ecosystem.

Areas of Focus:

BuiltWorlds Startup Profiles covers the complete built world value chain: PreCon, Procurement, Vertical & Horizontal Builds, Building Tech, Infrastructure Tech, and Climate Tech.

BuiltWorlds Venture research is broken down into three categories: ConTech, Infrastructure Tech, & Building Tech.

For a better understanding of BuiltWorlds 3 Venture categories → Click here

To Checkout BuiltWorlds' 2023 Annual Venture Report → Click here

Membership Access Levels:

  • Enterprise+ members have the right to request up to three startup profiles per quarter.
  • Strategic members have the right to request up to nine startup profiles per quarter.

A startup profile can include the following information:

Overview

  • Year Launched
  • Location
  • Building Stage & Specialty Area
  • Headcount
  • Founder(s)
  • Company URL
  • Overview of Product/Services

Business Model

  • Revenue Generator(s)
  • Product Focus
  • Largest Customers
  • Strategic Partnerships

Leadership Evaluation

  • Leadership Overview
  • Name & Position of Founder(s)
  • Headshot
  • Education
  • Applicable Work Experience

Funding Status 

  • Funding to Date
  • Previous Funding
  • Current/Target Funding
  • Planned Utilization of Funds

Market Opportunity & Risk

  • Target Market
  • Market Opportunity
  • Competitive Advantage (Management Assessment)
  • Risk Factors
  • Competitors

Insights: BuiltWorlds Benchmarking Survey

  • Relevant Data/Graphics from BuiltWorlds Benchmarking Program
  • Link to associated Report
  • Assessment of Company Given Data Provided

Intellectual Property & Awards

  • Technology & IP Status
  • Award(s) the Founder(s) and/or Company Has Received

Startup Profiles below are password protected, and available to BuiltWorlds Enterprise+ & Strategic Members only. Members can request reports by completing the form in the button below. The reports listed below are all of the currently available reports you are able to choose from. 

If your company is not a member, you can complete the form, and a member of the team will contact you to review the membership option that is best for your business.
