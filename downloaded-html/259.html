Smart Built Environment Forum

4th UAE Edition
3 – 4 October 2023

The Smart Built Environment Forum, Dubai, is the annual meeting place for powerful stakeholders who lead the management of the built environment.

In the past three years, this platform has successfully brought together five major built industry sectors under one roof in its previous iterations.

The 4th Edition of the Smart Built Environment Forum, Dubai aims to expand conversations to include urban planners, architectural firms, real-estate developers, construction consultants, FM businesses, community management and owner’s association representatives, property & asset management firms, and PropTech platforms.

The Theme for the 2023 edition is Artificial Intelligence and the Built Environment.

Macro Themes

Smart Environment

Smart living experiences that are empowering, effective and give a sense of ownership offer a sense of belonging to a “smart community.” Our cities are only as smart as our people, and their opportunities as co-creators of their experiences create “smart connectedness.”

Artificial Intelligence

Artificial generative intelligence can prove to be a boon or a bane to the built industry. From threatening to make certain tasks and roles obsolete to powering business efficiency, the evolution of ChatGPT’s capabilities signifies immense change for the built environment.

Sustainability

A status check on the commitment to be Net-Zero by 2050. The discussions around this will also involve the challenges and pitfalls that are hampering acceleration towards decarbonising the built environment. A more elaborate conversation will discuss relevant solutions and technologies.
