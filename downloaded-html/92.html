What we look for

Robust technologies and value propositions

We avoid hyped-up sectors, we love tangible solutions, prototypes, and successful use cases. Always looking for ambitious, breakthrough technologies with unique competitive advantages. Let your product do the talking.

Passion and
Enthusiasm

We partner with companies founded by enthusiastic, visionary, and passionate entrepreneurs who believe they can change the world and who are driven by a powerful desire to build concrete value and innovate entire industries.

Complementary and diverse teams

We are strong believers in teamwork and back teams that share diverse experiences, know-how, and backgrounds. Innovators are the ones that combine perspectives to develop ideas that challenge the status quo.
