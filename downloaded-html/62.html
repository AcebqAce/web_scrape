A list of Australian Venture Capital firms

Last updated February 16 2023

Are you looking for a list of Australian venture capital firms? Maybe you are looking for investors for your startup, maybe you are looking for a VC job, or maybe you are just interested. No matter what, we have you covered.

Below is a list of 135 Venture Capital Funds that invest in Australia. Not all of these funds have offices in Australia — if you only want to include investors with an office presence, click here. If you want to narrow the search even further to only show funds that are headquartered in Australia, click here.

The investors are sorted according to user rating. Alternatively, you can sort by size, age or alphabetically. Looking for other fund types? See our popular pages on private equity funds, accelerator programs, or angel and seed groups or choose another type using the Fund Type filter below.

If you have a more specific query, please visit our dedicated page for Venture Capital Investors in Australia/NZ (Oceania) where you have full access to all of our powerful search filters. If you want to understand the Australian venture capital industry in more detail, we recommend you have a read of our recent article covering off the formation and growth of the Australian VC industry. If you are looking for statistics including the number of funds, the average fund size, or other data, you can also visit our Australian Venture Capital dataset, or our Global Venture Capital dataset.
