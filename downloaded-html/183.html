Deutsche Invest Capital Partners (DICP) is a privately held alternative asset manager with more than €4.0B of assets under management across four investment strategies: capital solutions, special situations, technology and real estate.

Founded in 2006 in Munich, DICP is a trusted entrepreneurial and solution-oriented partner for investors and entrepreneurs in its core geography Germany, Austria and Switzerland (DACH region).

DICP is committed to creating superior value for its investors by identifying, realizing and executing high quality transactions, continuously supporting portfolio companies in their value creation until exit. In all its focus investment strategies, capital solutions, special situations, technology and real estate, DICP is driven by an entrepreneurial, independent and creative mindset, deep expertise, a disciplined value generation approach and a strong global network. DICP’s team combines highly experienced investment professionals, sector specialists and operating executives.

At DICP, we are passionate about growth and committed to highest performance standards.

DICP invests in businesses, ideas and situations to which we can add true value across multiple disciplines. We build strong relationships with ambitious management teams and (co-)investors alike.

As an alternative multi-asset manager, DICP’s approach hinges on its deep and extensive network across the DACH region combined with excellent execution skills, strong value-creation capabilities and a targeted investment style. This combination provides unfettered access to investment opportunities with attractive risk-return ratios in form of majority and protected minority holdings.
