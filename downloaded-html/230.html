A Tech-Enabled Circular Economy

Innovative digital technologies are poised to modernize, optimize and transform supply chains to eliminate waste

Our Vision

At Closed Loop Partners, we recognize technology as a key enabler for the circular economy. To eliminate waste, we first need to understand the flow of materials through our economy; where products are sourced, how they’re transported, and where they go after use. Through innovative digital technologies, we can better track materials so that they remain in play. 

Our Approach 

The opportunities for transformative applications of digital technologies are extensive, spanning industries from fashion to recycling to food, and we are harnessing the power of artificial intelligence and machine learning, among others, to advance circularity. Data streams, analytic platforms, and predictive approaches can improve circular economy decision-making and help usher in the new sustainable and profitable economic model.
