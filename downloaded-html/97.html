About

3TS – A Leading European Tech Growth Capital Investor

3TS Capital Partners is one of the leading European technology focused private equity and venture capital firms. 3TS provides expansion capital and buyout funding for small and medium-sized businesses in growth sectors including Technology & Internet, Media & Communications and Technology-Enabled Services. Investors in the current and past 3TS funds totaling over €400 million include EIF, Erste Group, Tesi, Cisco, OTP, EBRD, AWS, Sitra, KfW, and 3i among others.

Our team of highly qualified experts become active business partners of the portfolio companies and deliver substantial value added beyond financing. When analyzing new investment opportunities and supporting portfolio companies, we often augment our core team by our sector partners, various co-investors and other industry experts. 3TS is also an active member of several international industry associations and other organizations like – Invest Europe.

INVESTMENTS

Sample Current and Past Technology, Communications and Services Investments

3TS primarily targets investments in the broader TMT sectors including Technology & Internet (Software, Hardware, Mobile, Ecommerce, etc.), Media & Communications (Digital Media, Operators, etc.) and Technology-Enabled Services (Consumer and Business Services, Healthcare Services, etc.). We focus on exceptional high growth SMEs, which are either proven businesses becoming local leaders or truly innovative global challengers. As a stage-agnostic investor we typically invest in the range of €300,000 to €10 million to finance expansion plans, acquisitions and buyouts.

Team

Entrepreneurial Investors and Active Business Partners

Over the last two decades the 3TS team has built a solid track record of entrepreneurial investing across Europe and globally. Today 3TS has deep experience in sourcing opportunities, leading complex transactions, supporting companies, working with co-investors and realizing value. To date 3TS has made over 50 investments and successfully exited more than half of the portfolio companies providing substantial positive net returns. Beyond providing funding 3TS experts become active business partners for the founders and the managers of the portfolio companies delivering substantial value-added through, for example, best practices, knowledge transfer, business development and global partner networks.
