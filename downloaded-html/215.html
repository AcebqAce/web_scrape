CLIMATETECH IS WHO WE ARE:

The road to net zero runs through the built environment and stretches from farm to fork, with infinite investment potential due to the endless resources required to sustainably shelter and feed the Earth’s 7.9 billion inhabitants. To deal with the Climate Crisis, we must address the challenges in the Real Estate, Agriculture and Food Sectors.

Our portfolio companies enable smart and efficient use of energy, water and land in the agriculture, food, and real estate sectors. We are partnering with entrepreneurs to build their businesses, bringing decades of experience as investors with excellent results.

Our activities are concentrated in two investment funds: Agro & Food Tech and Building Innovation.

LOCATIONS

Greensoil Investments is based in Toronto, Ontario, Canada and Ra’anana, Israel – chosen strategically for the impressive amount of innovation happening in these countries. Both Canada and Israel are hotbeds of venture capital and private equity investment in ClimateTech. Toronto is now the third-largest tech hub in North America, trailing after New York and the Silicon Valley, while Israel has long been valued for its thriving innovation ecosystem and entrepreneurial capabilities.
