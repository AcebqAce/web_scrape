Accelerating the Circular Fashion Economy

At Closed Loop Partners we believe that a sustainable and circular future for fashion offers resilience and efficiencies, as well as protecting the environment we share.

New supply chain pressures, changing consumer values, technological developments and regulatory forces are transforming the landscape. And there is a meaningful investment opportunity in the realignment and modernization of the industry.

Our Approach

At every stage of the fashion supply chain, there are opportunities for reimagining how we make, sell, and consume products. We are exploring smart planning systems, reuse and resell technologies, low impact material science, IoT and blockchain solutions to track a product’s life cycle and advanced recycling technologies to ensure that clothing gets a second, third or infinite lease on life. With the right kind of investment, collaboration and innovation, we can address the challenges of today while preparing for a better, more sustainable and profitable tomorrow.
