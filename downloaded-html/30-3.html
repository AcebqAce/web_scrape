Senseforce

Senseforce is a machine data management solution helping machine manufacturers tackle Industry 4.0 challenges.

http://www.senseforce.io

Senseforce.io was founded in 2016 to provide industrial machinery manufacturers with a predictive maintenance platform for the connected machines they produce. They built a real-time edge-cloud data warehouse for industrial Internet of Things (IoT) helping manufacturers close the data gap between machine sellers and buyers. This enables the machine industry to build service-based business models like machine as a service, predictive maintenance as a service, and more. Among their customers are leading companies like Künz (heavy lift cranes and hydropower sector) and IMA Schelling Group (woodworking and metal processing industry) who made Senseforce part of their core digital strategy.

Kianava

Europe’s first full-stack chronic care provider applying integrative medicine.

https://kianava.de/

Kianava delivers multidisciplinary, holistic medical coaching online. Unique in the world of European virtual care providers, Kianava helps people with chronic conditions win back their health and well-being with a holistic, whole-body therapeutic plan delivered by a multidisciplinary team of experts. This move away from siloed medical treatment allows for the combination of the latest in standard medicine with evidence-based behavioral approaches, such as mind-body medicine and a strong focus on sleep, nutrition and stress management.
