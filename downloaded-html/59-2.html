Founder success stories

Our portfolio companies have made exceptional achievements both financially and in terms of positive impact.
Offers grid flexibility and congestion services, and developed a layer of software that can be implemented with any battery to provide more efficient usage.
Sympower is a demand-response specialist that uses flexibility to power the energy transition. The team raised a €5.2 m Series B round to enable the shift from fossil fuels to renewables.
Offers integrated technical and financial solutions that enable local smart energy networks through long-lasting storage. Starke Energy offers the integrated technical and financial solutions that enable local smart energy networks.
Fibersail is a shape sensing system based on FBG fiber optic research and development to monitor and analyze wind turbine blades in terms of shape, condition, and behavior. The company helps wind turbines to improve performance +4%.
FlexiDAO is a frontrunner in providing intelligence for 24/7 carbon-free energy tracking and is transforming the way energy retailers manage data to unlock new digital and green energy services.
With its AI-powered software platform, Dexter predicts energy supplies and demands as well as market movements. They raised a €2 million Series A round to accelerate the energy transition.
