Focus
We initially invest € 0.5 to 2m in seed-stage tech- and data-driven B2B software companies across Europe with global ambition. Feel free to read our detailed investment thesis.
Background
We are seasoned entrepreneurs turned investors. With Hybris and eCircle we previously built two of Europe's largest B2B software companies culminating in $1.6b cash exits to SAP and Teradata respectively.
Approach
We aim to be the investors we wished we had back then as founders: Contributing the product- and go-to-market experience we've garnered as founders and later senior executives to help scale B2B software companies through all stages. And be sparring partners in developing the founders.
