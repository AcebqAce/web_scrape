Data sourced from Crunchbase and SemRush.

Tera Funding

Crunchbase | Website | Twitter | Facebook | Linkedin

Tera Funding is a real estate investment firm in Seoul, South Korea.

Bdsdiet

Crunchbase | Website | Twitter | Facebook | Linkedin

Bdsdiet is a technology-powered real estate brokerage that represents people buying and selling homes. Founded and run by technologists.

bdsdiet.com features all of the for-sale-by-owner properties. Bdsdiet real estate agents work with the company’s team of engineers to create tools that make the buying and selling process easier.

The service is launched in December 2014. Bdsdiet’s main competitors include real estate search services like Zigbang and Naver, and traditional real estate brokerages.

None of their competitors combine online real estate search/technology tools and brokerage services. Customers can find historical price data, apartment review written by neighbors and everything about apartments.

The Company was founded by Changwook Kim and is based in Seoul.. .

Apex Investment & Company

Crunchbase | Website | Twitter | Facebook | Linkedin

APEX Investment & Company is a private equity investment fund and strategic consulting firm based in Korea. From the private equity investment perspective, APEX specializes in leveraged buyouts and M&A in the traditional industry and is keeping an eye on the infrastructure, transportation, retail and real estate sectors.

The company has recently worked with the Korean national bank to explore the investment opportunities in Europe & the Middle East. Meanwhile, APEX has also extended its investment portfolio on the cutting-edge technologies in the field of ICT, cyber security and bio tech in both Asia and Europe..
