HOW WE ARE DIFFERENT

We are a sophisticated private investment firm offering founders operational support and privileged access to top global investors.

Our portfolio is exclusively for reputable and like-minded families and HNWIs.

Expeditions Fund is a long-term and patient venture capital fund investing in early-stage technology companies globally led by mission-driven founders.

The fund has initially started as venture capital operations of Firlej Kastory family office. In 2021, Firlej Kastory expanded beyond immediate family management and the venture capital firm, Expeditions Fund, was created.

Expeditions Fund today is an independent venture fund backed by multi-billion dollar investors, family offices, founders and CEOs of successful companies.

In each company we are looking at the four core elements:

Mission-driven Founders

We help people who dare to build something meaningful that goes beyond pure financial profit

Uniqueness

A company should have a compelling competitive advantage based on their technology and the plan of how to build a strong long-term moat

Enterprise-oriented

We prefer to invest in B2B SaaS companies

Targeting Big Market

Total Addressable Market over $1bn with a steady growth rate and favourable trends 

VALUE CREATION STRATEGY

We are a specialised group focused on particular stage and sectors where we have ability to add a significant value.

We extend our wider investment base to help founders overcome specific challenges, exchange knowledge, and learn from former technology founders.

Mikołaj Firlej
Co-founder, General Partner


Mikolaj has spent several years in venture capital and family office in London and Warsaw. He was also advising Netguru, a leading software development agency in the long-term strategy and acquisitions as well as he helped to establish a new academic Centre at the University of Oxford focused on exploring policy and regulatory challenges of frontier technologies. He is a Lecturer at the Surrey Institute for People-Centred Artificial Intelligence, and holds degrees from the University of Oxford and Warsaw in Law, Philosophy, and Public Policy. In his personal life, Mikolaj is supporting gifted schoolchildren, particularly from Beskid Mountains.

Stanisław Kastory
Co-founder, General Partner

Stanisław’s goal is to steer private wealth towards more impact driven approach. In his past he worked for the biggest Polish law firm – Domanski Zakrzewski Palinka (DZP) specializing in M&A advisory. Stanislaw established and led DZP Future to advise startups during a capital raising, restructuring, corporate disputes, and international expansion. Stanislaw holds an MA diploma in Law and Administration from Warsaw University. He dedicates his free time to his two biggest passions – alpine and freestyle skiing.

Zofia Krajewska
Investment Associate

Zofia works principally on investigating and analyzing potential investment opportunities and supporting value-creation activities in portfolio companies. She is finishing her BSc in International Business Administration at Erasmus University, and approaching her masters at Bocconi University. Her interests include psychology and nutrition, and she enjoys hiking and impressionist art.

Steve Ciesinski
Senior Advisor

Steve is a seasoned operator, investor, board member, and advisor to tech- and science-based companies in a broad range of industries. Steve was most recently president of Silicon Valley-based SRI International, one of the world’s leading independent R&D organizations.

Akshay Kiran
