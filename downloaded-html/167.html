Climate Tech VCs - The Global List

The climate tech VC scene has grown exponentially in the last years.

We captured an overview of climate VC funds segmented by geography and stage covered, then further narrowed by sub-area:

  • Climate first - covering all areas of climate

  • Deep tech - covering deep tech and overlapping with several areas of climate

  • Specific climate sector - covering a specific area of climate

  • Impact - social impact focus going beyond climate

 

Climate Tech VCs: The full list of xxx VC funds active in climate tech investing

For ease of use, we added two lists of climate tech VCs below:

List 1: Climate tech funds included in the TTI infographic

This list is divided by stage, geography and specific area of investment. Please note that there are some overlaps among these criteria, e.g. multiple stages or geographies covered.

List 2: All other funds with exposure to climate tech

This longer list also includes traditional VC funds with one/more climate tech companies in their current portfolio. We recreated this list through the 100s of submissions in the comment section of our founder Alessa Berg’s post about this TTI infographic. If your fund isn’t included and you have exposure to climate tech, you’re welcome to submit it here and we’ll make sure to include you.
